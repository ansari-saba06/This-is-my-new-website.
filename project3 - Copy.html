Imarti

Imarti is a popular Indian sweet, especially known in Uttar Pradesh, particularly in Jaunpur. It's made by deep-frying a batter of black gram flour in a circular, flower-like shape, then soaking it in sugar syrup. Jaunpur Imarti is a century-old from the Jaunpur district of Uttar Pradesh.
